Why Value + Manual?

The "Nice Ticks" Problem

ECharts' type: 'time' algorithm favors human-readable intervals over developer intent. If it decides 4 ticks look "nicer" than 3, it will silently override your configuration.

"Result: Non-deterministic UI layouts."

The Manual Advantage

By treating the axis as a mathematical range (0-100 or Timestamp), we "anchor" labels to specific points: [min, mid, max].

We use a Custom Formatter to map numeric positions back to calendar months with 100% certainty.
